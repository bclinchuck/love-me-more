love me more

project by brandon linchuck

the world needs more love and kindness. love me more is a daily question, designed to spark self-reflection and cultivate self-love. it's a small moment each day just for you.

this project helps remember the little things in life, the things we often overlook that can bring us joy and gratitude when we take a moment to appreciate them. it's a reminder that you are worthy. self-reflection can be a powerful tool for growth and happiness. you reign yourself. love yourself more, and the world will be a little brighter
